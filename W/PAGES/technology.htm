TECHNOLOGY OF ISRO

Technology

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has pioneered advanced space technology, revolutionizing satellite communication, Earth observation, and interplanetary exploration. ISRO's Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) and Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle (GSLV) have established India as a major spacefaring nation, successfully launching satellites for various purposes, including navigation, remote sensing, and weather forecasting. The organization has also developed cutting- edge cryogenic engines, enabling powerful launches for deep-space missions. ISRO's NavIC system, an indigenous navigation technology, enhances India's capabilities in positioning and timing services. With innovations like reusable launch vehicles (RLV) and advancements in space-based solar observatories, ISRO continues to push technological boundaries, contributing to global scientific progress.
