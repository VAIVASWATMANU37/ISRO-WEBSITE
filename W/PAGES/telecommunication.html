TELECOMMUNICATION-SATELLITES OF ISRO

Telecommunication-Satellites

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has significantly advanced India's telecommunications infrastructure through its INSAT and GSAT satellite series. These satellites provide reliable broadcasting, telephony, internet connectivity, and emergency communication services across the country. The GSAT-24, GSAT-30, and GSAT-31 satellites enhance high-speed data transmission and support digital connectivity. Additionally, ISRO's NavIC system strengthens navigation-based communication, benefiting industries like aviation and maritime. With continuous innovations in high-throughput satellite technology, ISRO ensures seamless communication services, bridging gaps in remote and underserved regions.
