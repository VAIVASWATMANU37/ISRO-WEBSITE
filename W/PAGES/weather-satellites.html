WEATHER-SATELLITES OF ISRO

Weather-Satellites

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has developed advanced weather satellites to enhance climate monitoring, disaster prediction, and meteorological research. The INSAT series provides crucial real-time data for monsoon tracking, cyclone forecasting, and rainfall prediction, helping mitigate the impact of extreme weather events. The RISAT satellites, equipped with radar imaging, support cloud mapping and disaster response, even in challenging atmospheric conditions. SCATSAT-1, specializing in ocean wind monitoring, improves cyclone warnings and maritime safety. These satellites contribute to global climate studies, ensuring accurate weather forecasting and protecting lives and infrastructure.
