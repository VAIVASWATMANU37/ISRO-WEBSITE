SPACEPORTS OF ISRO

SPACEPORTS

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) operates key spaceports that serve as launch sites for India's ambitious space missions. The primary facility, Satish Dhawan Space Centre (SDSC) in Sriharikota, is India's premier launch site, handling launches of PSLV, GSLV, and SSLV rockets. Strategically located on the east coast, it enables efficient satellite deployment into various orbits. ISRO is also developing a second spaceport in Kulasekarapattinam, Tamil Nadu, aimed at enhancing launch capabilities, particularly for small satellite missions. These spaceports play a crucial role in India's growing presence in global space exploration, supporting missions ranging from Earth observation to interplanetary exploration.
