MARS-MISSION OF ISRO

Mars-Mission

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) made history with its Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan) , launched in 2013, making India the first country to reach Mars on its maiden attempt. This low-cost, highly efficient interplanetary mission provided valuable insights into the Martian atmosphere, surface features, and methane presence. The mission demonstrated ISRO's expertise in deep-space navigation and planetary exploration, solidifying India's position in global space research. As ISRO looks ahead, future Mars missions aim to study the planet's geology, climate, and potential for habitability, reinforcing India's role in interplanetary exploration.
