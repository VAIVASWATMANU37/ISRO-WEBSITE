SUSTAINABILITY PROGRAMS OF ISRO

Sustainability

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is committed to long-term sustainability in space activities, ensuring responsible exploration while minimizing environmental impact. ISRO actively participates in global discussions on space debris management, sustainable satellite operations, and eco-friendly propulsion systems. Through initiatives like the Indian Space Situational Assessment Report (ISSAR) 2024, ISRO monitors space assets and enhances compliance with international sustainability guidelines. The organization also focuses on green building concepts, renewable energy, and waste recycling at its facilities to reduce its carbon footprint. Additionally, ISRO collaborates with the United Nations Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space (COPUOS) to promote equitable access to space for future generations.
