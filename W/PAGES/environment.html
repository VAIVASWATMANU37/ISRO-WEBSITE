ENVIORNMENTAL PLANS BY ISRO

Enviornmental Plans

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) actively contributes to environmental sustainability through its remote sensing and Earth observation programs. ISRO's satellites, such as IRS, Cartosat, and RISAT, provide crucial data for monitoring deforestation, climate change, and disaster management. The organization also conducts research on atmospheric studies, oceanography, and biodiversity conservation, supporting global environmental initiatives. Additionally, ISRO collaborates with institutions like the Indian Institute of Remote Sensing (IIRS) to develop advanced technologies for environmental monitoring and resource management. Through these efforts, ISRO plays a vital role in promoting ecological balance and sustainable development.
