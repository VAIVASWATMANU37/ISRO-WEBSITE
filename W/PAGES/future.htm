FUTURE PLANS OF ISRO

Future

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is shaping an ambitious future with groundbreaking missions and technological advancements. A key upcoming project is Gaganyaan, India's first human spaceflight program, which aims to send astronauts into low Earth orbit. The Aditya-L1 mission will study the Sun, helping scientists understand solar activity and its effects on space weather. ISRO is also working on Chandrayaan-4, advancing lunar exploration beyond previous missions. Additionally, plans for interplanetary missions to Venus and beyond are in motion, along with space-based observatories for astrophysics research. With innovations in Reusable Launch Vehicles (RLV) and satellite constellations, ISRO continues to expand India's presence in global space exploration, reinforcing its position as a leader in cutting-edge technology.
