QUANTUM TECH OF ISRO

Quantum-Tech

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is making strides in quantum technology, particularly in Quantum Key Distribution (QKD), which ensures ultra-secure satellite communication. ISRO successfully demonstrated free-space QKD over 300 meters, a major milestone toward Satellite-Based Quantum Communication (SBQC). Under India's National Quantum Mission (NQM), ISRO is working on quantum encryption, quantum materials, and secure inter-city quantum networks. These advancements will enhance cybersecurity, defense applications, and deep-space communication, reinforcing India's leadership in quantum research.
