AI DEVELOPMENT BY ISRO

Artificial Intelligence

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) into various aspects of space technology, enhancing efficiency and automation. ISRO is developing 50 AI-powered satellites designed for surveillance and geo-intelligence gathering, set to be launched over the next five years. AI also plays a crucial role in satellite data processing, spacecraft operations, and launch vehicle management, improving mission accuracy and decision-making. Additionally, ISRO is leveraging machine learning algorithms for aircraft detection, disaster monitoring, and deep-space exploration, reinforcing India's leadership in AI-driven space advancements.
