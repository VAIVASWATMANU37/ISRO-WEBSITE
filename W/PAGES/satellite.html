SATELLITES OF ISRO

SATELLITES

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has launched a diverse range of satellites, each serving vital roles in communication, navigation, remote sensing, and scientific exploration. ISRO's INSAT series supports weather forecasting and telecommunications, while the IRS (Indian Remote Sensing) satellites provide crucial Earth observation data for agriculture, disaster management, and environmental studies. The NavIC constellation ensures precise navigation for various applications, competing with global systems. Scientific missions like Chandrayaan, Mangalyaan, and Astrosat have expanded India's capabilities in planetary exploration and space research. Additionally, ISRO has developed satellites like RISAT and Cartosat, which aid in strategic surveillance and high-resolution mapping. With continuous advancements, ISRO is shaping India's future in space technology and exploration.
