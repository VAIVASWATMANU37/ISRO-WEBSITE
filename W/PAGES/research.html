REASEARCH OF ISRO

REASEARCH

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is at the forefront of cutting-edge scientific research, advancing space technology, satellite applications, and interplanetary exploration. ISRO conducts extensive studies in remote sensing, astrophysics, planetary science, and satellite communication, contributing to India's progress in space-based applications. The organization has developed indigenous innovations like the NavIC satellite navigation system and cryogenic propulsion technology, enhancing India's capabilities in space. ISRO's upcoming research focuses on lunar and Mars exploration, deep-space missions, and solar studies through the Aditya-L1 mission. Through collaborations with global space agencies and scientific institutions, ISRO continues to push the boundaries of space research, cementing India’s position in the global space community.
