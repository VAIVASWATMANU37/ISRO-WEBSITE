OUTREACH OF ISRO

Outreach

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) actively engages in outreach programs to inspire and educate the public about space science and technology. Through initiatives like student satellite projects, workshops, and public exhibitions, ISRO nurtures young minds and encourages innovation. The organization collaborates with universities, research institutions, and schools, offering internships, scholarships, and training programs for aspiring scientists. ISRO also conducts live streaming of rocket launches, allowing people worldwide to witness India's advancements in space exploration. With interactive platforms, educational campaigns, and scientific awareness programs, ISRO continues to bridge the gap between space technology and society, fostering curiosity and scientific growth in future generations
