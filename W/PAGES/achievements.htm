ACHIEVEMENTS OF ISRO

Achievements

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has achieved remarkable milestones, cementing India's reputation in global space exploration. One of its greatest successes was Chandrayaan-1, which confirmed the presence of water molecules on the Moon. The Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan) made India the first country to reach Mars on its maiden attempt, with one of the most cost-effective interplanetary missions ever. ISRO's PSLV and GSLV rockets have launched numerous satellites, supporting communication, navigation, and Earth observation. The NavIC system provides precise positioning services, strengthening India's technological independence. Future projects like Gaganyaan, India's first human spaceflight mission, and Aditya-L1, a solar exploration mission, reflect ISRO's commitment to pushing boundaries in space science.
