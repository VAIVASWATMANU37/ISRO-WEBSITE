ISRO'S MISSIONS

Missions

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has played a pivotal role in advancing space technology and exploration. With a commitment to innovation, ISRO has successfully launched numerous missions that havstrengthened India's position in the global space community. Chandrayaan (India's lunar mission) brought significant discoveries about water on the Moon, while Mangalyaan (Mars Orbiter Mission) made India the first country to reach Mars in its first attempt. ISRO's PSLV and GSLV rockets have propelled satellites into various orbits, supporting communications, navigation, and Earth observation. Future missions like Gaganyaan, India's first human spaceflight mission, and Aditya-L1, a solar exploration mission, continue to push boundaries. Through these endeavors, ISRO remains a beacon of scientific excellence and technological advancement.
