The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) provides multiple channels for communication and inquiries, ensuring accessibility for researchers, students, and space enthusiasts. ISRO's headquarters is located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, overseeing strategic decisions and mission planning. Various ISRO centers, including Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre (VSSC), Satish Dhawan Space Centre (SDSC), and the U R Rao Satellite Centre (URSC), manage different aspects of India's space missions. ISRO maintains an official website https://www.isro.gov.in where users can explore updates, mission details, career opportunities, and outreach programs. The organization actively engages with the public through its social media platforms, offering insights into space research, satellite launches, and educational collaborations.
