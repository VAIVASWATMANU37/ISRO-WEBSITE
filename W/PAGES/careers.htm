CAREER PLANS IN ISRO

CAREERS

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) offers exciting career opportunities for individuals passionate about space science and technology. As one of the world's leading space agencies, ISRO recruits talented engineers, scientists, and technicians to work on groundbreaking missions in satellite development, space exploration, and rocket technology. It provides roles in fields such as aerospace engineering, astrophysics, remote sensing, robotics, and artificial intelligence, contributing to India's advancements in space research. Through programs like young scientist internships, graduate trainee programs, and research fellowships, ISRO nurtures future space innovators. With a commitment to excellence, ISRO continues to attract skilled professionals dedicated to expanding humanity's reach beyond Earth.
