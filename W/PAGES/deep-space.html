DEEP-SPACE REASEARCH OF ISRO

Deep-Space Reasearch

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has made significant advancements in deep-space exploration, pushing the boundaries of planetary science and interstellar research. Missions like Chandrayaan, Mangalyaan, and Astrosat have provided valuable insights into the Moon, Mars, and distant cosmic phenomena. ISRO's Aditya-L1 mission is focused on studying the Sun’s influence on space weather, while future projects aim to explore Venus and beyond. With advancements in cryogenic propulsion, space observatories, and interplanetary communication, ISRO continues to strengthen India's position in global deep-space missions, contributing to astronomical discoveries and expanding human knowledge of the universe.
