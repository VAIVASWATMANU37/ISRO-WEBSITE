LAUCH VEHICLES OF ISRO

Launch-Vehicles

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has developed a range of launch vehicles that have propelled India's space ambitions to new heights. The Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) is one of the most successful and versatile rockets, known for launching satellites into various orbits with precision. The Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle (GSLV), equipped with cryogenic engines, enables heavier payload launches, supporting deep-space missions like Chandrayaan and Mangalyaan. The Small Satellite Launch Vehicle (SSLV) has been introduced for cost-effective, rapid deployment of smaller satellites. ISRO is also working on Reusable Launch Vehicles (RLV) and Next-Generation Heavy Lift Rockets, paving the way for advanced space exploration and interplanetary missions.
