DISASTER MANAGEMENT BY ISRO

Disaster Management

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) plays a crucial role in disaster management by leveraging space-based technology to monitor and mitigate natural disasters. Through its Disaster Management Support (DMS) Programme, ISRO provides real-time satellite data for tracking cyclones, floods, landslides, earthquakes, forest fires, and droughts. Satellites like INSAT, RISAT, and Cartosat help in early warning systems, damage assessment, and emergency response planning. ISRO also collaborates with global initiatives such as the International Charter 'Space & Major Disasters' to provide satellite-based disaster relief worldwide. By integrating remote sensing, communication, and navigation technologies, ISRO enhances India's ability to manage crises effectively, protecting lives and infrastructure.
