ROBOT DEVELOPMENT BY ISRO

Robotics

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is advancing robotics technology for space exploration, with a focus on autonomous systems and humanoid robots. A key development is Vyommitra, a humanoid robot designed for the Gaganyaan mission, which will simulate astronaut functions in an uncrewed test flight scheduled for December 2025. ISRO is also fostering innovation through the ISRO Robotics Challenge (IRoC-U2025), encouraging students to design robotic rovers for future planetary missions. These advancements in robotics will play a crucial role in lunar exploration, interplanetary missions, and space station operations, reinforcing India's position in global space technology.
