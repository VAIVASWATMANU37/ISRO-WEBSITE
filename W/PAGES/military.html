MILLATARY-SATELITES OF ISRO

Millatary-Satellites

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) plays a vital role in supporting India's defense ' capabilities through advanced satellite technology. ISRO has developed Earth observation satellites like RISAT and Cartosat, which provide high-resolution imagery for strategic surveillance and security operations. Additionally, ISRO's SpaDeX (Space Docking Experiment) has opened new frontiers for military applications, enabling on-orbit maintenance and refueling of defense satellites, enhancing India's space-based security infrastructure. These advancements strengthen India's ability to monitor borders, track threats, and support national security through cutting-edge space technology.
