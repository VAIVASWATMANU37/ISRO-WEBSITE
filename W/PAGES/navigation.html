NAVIGATIONAL-SATELLITES OF ISRO

Navigational-Satellites

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has developed NavIC (Navigation with Indian Constellation), an advanced regional satellite navigation system designed to provide precise positioning services across India and surrounding regions. NavIC consists of seven satellites, three in geostationary orbit and four in geosynchronous orbit, ensuring continuous coverage. It offers Standard Positioning Service (SPS) for civilian applications and Restricted Service (RS) for strategic users, enhancing India's independence in navigation technology. Additionally, ISRO has collaborated on GAGAN (GPS Aided Geo Augmented Navigation), improving accuracy for aviation and maritime operations. With ongoing advancements, ISRO continues to refine its navigation systems, ensuring reliable and high-precision location services for diverse applications.
