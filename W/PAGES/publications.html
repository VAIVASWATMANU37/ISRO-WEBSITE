PUBLICATIONS OF ISRO

Publications

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) publishes a wide range of scientific and technical documents that contribute to space research and innovation. Through research papers, reports, and journals, ISRO shares advancements in satellite technology, planetary exploration, astrophysics, and remote sensing. Publications like Mission Updates, Annual Reports, and Technical Reviews provide insights into ongoing projects, upcoming missions, and breakthroughs in space science. ISRO also releases educational material to promote space awareness among students and researchers. With a commitment to transparency and knowledge sharing, ISRO's publications serve as valuable resources for scientists, engineers, and space enthusiasts worldwide.
