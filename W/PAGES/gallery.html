GALLERY OF ISRO

Gallery

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has an extensive gallery showcasing India's remarkable journey in space exploration. From breathtaking images of Chandrayaan and Mangalyaan missions to high-resolution satellite imagery of Earth, ISRO's gallery highlights key achievements in planetary research, launch events, and deep-space observations. It features iconic moments like the PSLV and GSLV rocket launches, astronaut training for upcoming human spaceflight missions, and stunning visuals from the Aditya-L1 solar mission. ISRO's space imagery contributes to global scientific studies, environmental monitoring, and technological advancements, inspiring future generations in space science.
