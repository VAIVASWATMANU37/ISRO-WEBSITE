1JZ-GTE

The Toyota 1JZ-GTE is a legendary 2.5-liter inline-six turbocharged petrol engine from the JZ family, produced between 1990 and 2007. Revered by tuners and enthusiasts alike, it’s known for its robust cast-iron block, aluminum DOHC head, and immense tuning potential. The first generation featured twin CT12A turbochargers, delivering up to 280 PS and 363 Nm of torque, while the second generation adopted VVT-i and a single CT15B turbo, boosting low-end torque to 378 Nm. Its short stroke and high-revving nature make it ideal for performance builds, and it’s been the heart of iconic models like the Toyota Chaser Tourer V, Mark II, Soarer, and Supra MK III. Despite its age, the 1JZ-GTE remains a favorite for engine swaps and drift builds due to its durability, smooth power delivery, and aftermarket support. Just keep an eye on ceramic turbo wear, VVT-i valve issues, and ignition coil failures, especially in high-mileage units.
