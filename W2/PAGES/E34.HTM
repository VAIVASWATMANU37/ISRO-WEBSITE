Peugeot TU5JP4

The Peugeot TU5JP4 is a 1.6-liter inline-four petrol engine that was produced from 2000 to 2015 and widely used across various Peugeot and Citroën models, including the 206, 307, Partner, and Citroën C3 and Xsara Picasso. Known for its durable cast iron block and aluminum 16-valve cylinder head, the TU5JP4 features a BOSCH multiport fuel injection system and hydraulic lifters for valve clearance adjustment. It delivers between 90 to 110 horsepower and offers a torque range of 130 to 150 Nm, making it a reliable and efficient choice for compact vehicles. While generally robust, it’s prone to electrical glitches, ignition coil failures, and timing belt issues, which require regular maintenance to avoid costly repairs. Despite these quirks, the TU5JP4 remains a popular and well-regarded engine for its balance of performance, simplicity, and longevity.
