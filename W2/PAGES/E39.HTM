Nissan RB25DETT

The so-called **Nissan RB25DETT** is a fan-created concept rooted in the legendary RB series, rather than an official factory designation. Based on the proven **RB25DET**, it features a 2.5-liter inline-six with DOHC and electronic fuel injection, beloved for its iron block strength and turbo-ready design. Tuners seeking a twin-turbo setup often retrofit the RB25DET with custom twin turbochargers—borrowing layout ideas from the RB26DETT—resulting in builds that offer smoother spool times and serious horsepower gains. While it’s never existed as an official Nissan engine, the RB25DETT is celebrated as a symbol of creative engineering within the enthusiast community, merging reliability with raw turbocharged flair.
