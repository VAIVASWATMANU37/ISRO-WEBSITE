GM Ecotec 2.0L LNF/LHU

The GM Ecotec 2.0L LNF and LHU engines are turbocharged inline-fours that marked a turning point in General Motorsâ€™ approach to performance and efficiency. Introduced in the late 2000s, these engines featured direct injection, forged internals, and a BorgWarner K04 turbocharger, delivering up to 270 hp and 400 Nm of torque in factory trim. The LNF debuted in sporty models like the Cobalt SS and Solstice GXP, while the LHU refined the formula with improved NVH characteristics and emissions compliance, powering vehicles like the Buick Regal GS. Known for their tuning potential and robust construction, both engines remain popular among enthusiasts seeking a balance of modern tech and old-school boost-friendly durability.
