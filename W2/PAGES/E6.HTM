Ford 2.3 L EcoBoost

The Ford 2.3L EcoBoost is a turbocharged inline-four engine that blends performance and efficiency in a compact package. Introduced in 2015, it’s a stroked version of the 2.0L EcoBoost and has powered a range of vehicles—from the Mustang EcoBoost and Focus RS to the Ford Ranger and Bronco. This engine features a twin-scroll turbocharger, direct fuel injection, and DOHC with Ti-VCT (Twin Independent Variable Cam Timing), delivering between 270 to 350 horsepower and up to 350 lb-ft of torque, depending on the application. Built with a high-pressure die-cast aluminum block, forged steel crankshaft, and lightweight pistons, it’s engineered for both strength and responsiveness. What makes the 2.3L EcoBoost stand out is its versatility—it’s equally at home in a sporty coupe or a rugged SUV. With a flat torque curve and quick spool-up, it offers spirited driving while maintaining respectable fuel economy. It’s a modern workhorse with a performance edge.
