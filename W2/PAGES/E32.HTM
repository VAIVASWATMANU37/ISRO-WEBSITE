Hyundai Theta II 2.0T

The Hyundai Theta II 2.0T is a turbocharged inline-four engine that blends performance and efficiency across a wide range of Hyundai and Kia vehicles. With outputs ranging from 245 to 275 horsepower, it features direct fuel injection, CVVT on both cams, and a twin-scroll turbocharger for responsive power delivery. Built with an aluminum block and head, it’s lightweight yet capable, especially in sportier models like the Veloster N and Elantra N. While early versions faced reliability concerns—such as oil flow issues and bearing wear—later iterations improved significantly. Tuners appreciate its potential to exceed 300 hp with bolt-on upgrades, making it a favorite for those seeking a balance of daily drivability and spirited performance.
