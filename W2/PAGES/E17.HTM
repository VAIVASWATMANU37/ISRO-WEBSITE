Dodge HEMI 6.2L Hellcat

The Dodge HEMI 6.2L Hellcat is a supercharged V8 engine that redefined modern American muscle with its outrageous power and unmistakable roar. Introduced in 2015, it’s part of Chrysler’s Gen III HEMI family and features a cast iron block, aluminum heads, and a massive twin-screw supercharger that helps it produce between 707 and 840 horsepower, depending on the variant. With a 9.5:1 compression ratio, forged internals, and sodium-filled exhaust valves, the Hellcat engine is built to handle extreme performance demands. It powers legends like the Dodge Challenger and Charger Hellcat, Durango SRT Hellcat, and even the RAM TRX, earning a reputation as the heart of Dodge’s horsepower revolution. More than just numbers, the Hellcat is a visceral experience—raw, loud, and unapologetically over-the-top.
