K20/K24SERIES

The Honda K20 and K24 engines are part of the renowned K-series, celebrated for their performance, reliability, and tuning potential. The K20 is a 2.0-liter inline-4 engine known for its high-revving nature and was a favorite in performance models like the Civic Type R and Acura RSX Type-S. In contrast, the K24 offers a larger 2.4-liter displacement, delivering more low-end torque and making it ideal for street builds and daily driving. Both engines feature DOHC architecture with Honda’s i-VTEC system, but the K24’s longer stroke and taller deck height give it a torque advantage, while the K20 excels in high-RPM horsepower. Enthusiasts often swap and hybridize components between the two to create the ultimate performance setup.
