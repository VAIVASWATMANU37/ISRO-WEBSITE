Toyota GR V6

TThe Toyota GR V6 engine family is a modern series of 60-degree V6 gasoline engines known for their durability, refinement, and versatility across a wide range of Toyota and Lexus vehicles. Introduced in the early 2000s, these engines feature an aluminum block and DOHC aluminum heads, with technologies like VVT-i, Dual VVT-i, and D-4S direct+port fuel injection depending on the variant. Displacements range from 2.5 to 4.0 liters, with power outputs from around 200 to over 300 horsepower. Notable members include the 1GR-FE (4.0L, used in trucks and SUVs), 2GR-FE/FKS/FSE (3.5L, found in sedans, minivans, and crossovers), and the 3GR/4GR variants for luxury applications.
