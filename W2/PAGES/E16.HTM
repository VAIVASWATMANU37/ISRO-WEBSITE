Ford Coyote 5.0L

The Ford Coyote 5.0L is a naturally aspirated V8 engine that has become a cornerstone of modern American performance. Introduced in 2011 for the Mustang GT and F-150, it features an aluminum block, dual overhead cams, and Twin Independent Variable Cam Timing (Ti-VCT), delivering a blend of high-revving excitement and everyday drivability. With power outputs ranging from 412 to 480+ horsepower across its generations, the Coyote is known for its broad torque curve, robust internals, and aftermarket-friendly design. Its modular architecture and forged components make it a favorite for tuning and engine swaps, while its smooth, linear power delivery keeps it civil on the street. Whether in a muscle car or a pickup, the 5.0L Coyote roars with modern muscle charm.
